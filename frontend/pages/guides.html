Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about OpenSource Compass and open source contribution.

What is OpenSource Compass?

OpenSource Compass is a beginner-friendly platform that helps contributors navigate open source programs, best practices, and real-world contribution workflows.

Do I need prior experience?

No. Many contributions involve documentation, design, testing, and beginner-friendly issues. Programming experience helps but is not mandatory.

How do I start contributing?

Start by reading the Guides section, pick a beginner-friendly issue, follow contribution guidelines, and submit a pull request.

Which programs are beginner-friendly?

Programs like Hacktoberfest, Social Winter of Code, and GirlScript Summer of Code are great starting points.

Where can I get help?

Most projects have active communities on GitHub, Discord, or Slack. You can also refer to our Resources and Guides pages.

Will my contributions remain after the competition?

Yes. All merged pull requests remain permanently on GitHub and continue to strengthen your profile and portfolio.
