About Outreachy

Outreachy is one of the most unique and inclusive open-source programs. It provides three-month paid internships specifically for people who face under-representation, systemic bias, or discrimination in the tech industry.

The Two Annual Cohorts

Outreachy runs twice a year, providing opportunities for contributors to participate in open-source internships.

Mid-Year Cohort

May to August
Applications usually open in January

End-of-Year Cohort

December to March
Applications usually open in August

Step-by-Step Guide to the Process

Follow these steps to successfully apply and participate in Outreachy.

1

Initial Application (The Eligibility Phase)

Before you look at projects, you must pass an initial screening.

  • The Essay: You will write four short essays about your background and the hurdles you've faced in tech.
  • The Goal: Outreach wants to know why you need this opportunity. Be honest and authentic.
  • Timeline: For the May cohort, this usually happens in January/February.
2

The Contribution Period (The Trial Phase)

If your initial application is approved, you become a "Finalist."

  • Pick a Project: You will browse a list of projects from organizations like Linux Kernel, Mozilla, GNOME, or Wikimedia.
  • Start Contributing: You must record at least one contribution (a code fix, documentation update, or design task) on the Outreach website to be eligible for the internship.
  • Communication: Mentors observe how you interact with the community during this month.
3

The Final Proposal

After contributing, you submit a formal project proposal to the organization you worked with. If selected, you are paired with a mentor for the full internship.

GSoC vs. Outreachy: Key Differences

Understanding the differences between Google Summer of Code and Outreachy can help you choose the right program.

Feature GSoC Outreachy
Eligibility Students or Beginners People facing under-representation
Requirement Formal technical proposal Technical proposal + mandatory contribution
Focus Project-centric Diversity and career-growth centric
Stipend Based on PPP (location) Fixed $7,000 USD for all

How to Prepare Right Now

Start preparing for Outreachy today with these actionable steps.

1. Check Your Eligibility

Visit the Outreach Eligibility page to ensure you qualify.

2. Improve Your Git Skills

Since you must contribute before being selected, you need to be comfortable with the command line and Git workflows.

3. Draft Your Essays

Start thinking about your journey in tech. What obstacles have you overcome? What are your career goals? Begin drafting your essays early.
